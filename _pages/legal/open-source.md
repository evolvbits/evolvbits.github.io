## Our Open Source Philosophy

EvolvBits develops and maintains open-source software intended to be useful,
transparent, and accessible to the developer community.

We believe that open-source software encourages collaboration,
learning, and innovation.

## Project Licenses

Each project published under the EvolvBits organization is released
under its own license.

The license for a project can always be found in the repository of that
project.

Open-source licenses apply only to the **source code** of the project
and do not grant permission to use the EvolvBits name, logo, or brand.

## Contributing

Community feedback is welcome.

You can contribute by:

- Reporting bugs
- Suggesting improvements
- Opening issues in project repositories

At this time, contributions may be limited while projects are evolving.
